The GEODI management page serves as the access point for project creation, setting modifications, permission arrangements, indexing, and other settings.

General features will be explained in this training.

The content is technical, and the duration is 45 minutes.

The scope of the training includes Project Wizard, Agent Management Panel, Indexing, User/Permission Management, Customizations, Dictionary and Recognizers, Maps, and Questions.

The trainings aim to provide a general idea. It is necessary to reinforce what is learned by using it. Some features specified within the scope of the training may not be included in the user's license.
