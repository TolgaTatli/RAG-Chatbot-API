The 'Discovery PK' package, version 1.0.0.13, is designed to extend the capabilities of GEODI Discovery and Classification through country-specific semantic recognizers. This particular package is specifically for Pakistan.

Installation of the 'Discovery PK' package is required after GEODI Discovery has been installed.

The 'Discovery PK' package for Pakistan includes a range of specific semantic recognizers. These recognizers are the Pakistan CNIC Recognizer, IMEI Recognizer, Pakistan NICOP Recognizer, Pakistan Raast ID Recognizer, Pakistan SRTN/SST Recognizer, Pakistan NTN Recognizer, Pakistan City Recognizer, and Pakistani Rupee Recognizer.
