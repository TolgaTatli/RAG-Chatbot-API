The training is designed for users of GEODI Data Classification tools. With these tools, users will be able to perform manual or automatic classification within Desktop and Microsoft Office software.

The participants for this training include Technical, Sales, User, and General personnel.

There are no prerequisite trainings required.

Supporting materials for the training include the GEODI Classifier Office and Other Add-ons User Guide, and a User Training Video.

The trainings aim to provide a general idea, and it is necessary to reinforce what is learned by using it.

Users should not hesitate to ask for support from the teams regarding topics they are unsure about or cannot perform.

Some features mentioned within the scope of the training may not be included in the user's license.
