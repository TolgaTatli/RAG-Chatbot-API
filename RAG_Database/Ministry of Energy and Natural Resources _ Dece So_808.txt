Dece Software offers solutions that include Search, Discover, Mask, and Classify, which are part of the GEODI Platform. The company also lists "Encrypt" as a capability and states its purpose as "We Protect Your Data."

GEODI provides enhanced classification features that allow users to categorize their documents, e-mails, and contents. This categorization can be performed according to their respective importance and privacy levels.

The Ministry of Energy and Natural Resources, along with all of its 14 sub-organizations, prefers GEODI for data classification. This preference is stated to help them comply with GDPR regulations.
