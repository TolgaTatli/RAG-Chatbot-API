DECE Software has been included in Gartner Hype Cycle Reports. This information was noted on January 27, 2021.

DECE Software develops disruptive and functional solutions for digital data users. These solutions achieve preeminent outcomes and attract attention from tech researchers and followers. Gartner is recognized as a well-known research and advisory company globally, which monitors technological innovations.

DECE Software's solutions are listed in Gartner's "Hype Cycle for Analytics and Business Intelligence, 2020" and "Hype Cycle for Customer Experience Analytics, 2020" reports.

Being included in a list that features major software vendors such as Esri, Microsoft, Qlik, and Mapbox is considered an achievement for DECE Software's solutions.

The Gartner reports can be accessed via the provided link: https://www.gartner.com/document/3988448?ref=solrAll&refval=264410291.

DECE Software plans to continue improving and spreading its solutions globally.
