GEODI results can be reordered.

Sorting options include `sırala:ad/adı/isim` or `orderby:name`, and `sırala:tarih` or `orderby:date`.

Sorting behaves differently on the İçerikler (Contents) and Kelimeler (Words) pages.

For Kelimeler (Words), an additional sorting option is available: `sırala:katman` or `orderby:layer`.
