The scope of the content involves understanding the discovery panel that comes with GEODI Discovery and interpreting its capabilities. The intended audience for this information is general.

Prerequisite trainings for this content include GEODI 101 and GEODI 102.

The contents cover an explanation of panel tools and an explanation of local, remote, PC, and VT sources.

Further contents include the interpretation of problematic content, interpretation of remote PC discovery results, and interpretation of duplicate content. Destruction Scripts are also part of the contents.

Additional contents involve making a report of the panels, general reports, and video content.

Users are advised to ask for support from the teams if they are unsure about a topic or unable to do something. It is also noted that some features may not be included in a user's license.
