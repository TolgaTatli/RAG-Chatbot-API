Country-specific semantic recognizers extend the capabilities of GEODI Discovery and Classification. This package, identified as Discovery BH version 1.0.0.3, is specifically for Bahrain. It should be installed after GEODI Discovery.

The Bahrain package includes several specific semantic recognizers: Bahrain Dinar Recognizer, Bahrain City Recognizer, Bahrain ID Recognizer, and Bahrain Passport Recognizer.
